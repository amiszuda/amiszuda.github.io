abstract: We re-determine the binary orbit of the single-line eclipsing binary V381
  Car. After subtracting the fitted eclipse light-curve, we performed a Fourier analysis
  in each Strömgren passband. We found 5 independent frequencies, and made mode identifications
  from the photometric observables and from the spectroscopic IPS diagrams. We identified
  three frequencies; $nu_1$ and $nu_2$ are prograde quadrupole modes, and $nu_4$ is
  a dipole zonal mode. Another two frequencies were most probably higher-degree modes.
  We constructed preliminary seismic models to fit the dominant frequency and to investigate
  instability of the whole frequency range observed.
